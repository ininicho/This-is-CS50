About Me

Hello there! Here is a small biography of me.


Hello there!


My name is Nicholaus Suprapto. People usually call me Nic. I will be 18 this year. I'm from Indonesia, a big nation right in the equator. I live in Jakarta, the crowdest city in Indonesia. I just graduated high school from Canisius Senior High School which is located right in the heart of Jakarta. This year, I will be studying in the University of Waterloo, Ontario, Canada.


I have a big passion in Mathematics and Computers. Mathematics is one of my strong points in school. My leadership experience made me a great problem solver. Furthermore, I have developed passion in Computers in these past years. Algorithms and data structures are very fascinating for me. CS50 introduced most of these concepts to me. However, I would love to study them deeper. Therefore, I am taking Honours Mathematics in Waterloo to deepen my understanding of the world of Mathematics and Computers.


My hobbies include volleyball and mountain hiking. I am part of my school's volleyball team. I play in the back as a libero. It is the most underated position in volleyball. We do most of the work but people do not really recognize us. Aside from playing volleyball, I love mountain hiking. Indonesia is an archipelago of volcanoes. Hence, there are plenty of mountains to hike. Mountain hiking might be tiring at first. But once we reach the top, all our hardwork pays off. It's pretty much similar in life. Hardwork always pays off.
